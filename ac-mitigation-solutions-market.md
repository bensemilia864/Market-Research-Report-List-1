<p><p>AC Mitigation Solutions encompass several market types aimed at reducing harmful alternating current effects in electrical systems. </p><p>Fault shielding involves protective barriers to prevent electrical faults from impacting equipment or personnel. Gradient control mats are installed on the ground to manage voltage gradients, enhancing safety in sensitive areas. Lumped grounding systems offer a compact design for effective grounding, especially in constrained spaces. Lastly, gradient control wire is used to mitigate voltage differences along surfaces, ensuring uniform voltage distribution and improved safety.</p></p>
