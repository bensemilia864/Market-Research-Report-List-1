<p><p>Acrylate adhesives in the medical market are primarily classified into two types: acrylic and cyanoacrylate adhesives. Acrylic adhesives offer flexibility and are ideal for bonding various medical devices, providing durability and resistance to environmental factors. In contrast, cyanoacrylate adhesives are known for their fast curing properties and strong bonding capabilities, making them suitable for wound closure and surgical applications. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring reliable and efficient medical device assembly and patient care solutions.</p></p>
