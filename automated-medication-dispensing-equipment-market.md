<p><p>The Automated Medication Dispensing Equipment market is categorized into two main types: centralized and decentralized dispensing equipment. Centralized dispensing equipment consolidates medication storage and dispensing in a central location, typically within a pharmacy, enhancing operational efficiency and control. In contrast, decentralized dispensing equipment places medication dispensers closer to patient care areas, such as nursing units, improving access and reducing wait times for medication retrieval. Both types aim to enhance medication safety, streamline workflows, and enhance patient care delivery.</p></p>
