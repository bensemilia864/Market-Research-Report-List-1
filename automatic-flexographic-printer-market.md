<p><strong>The Automatic Flexographic Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unit -type Machine</li><li>Central Impression Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Flexographic Printer market comprises two main types: Unit-type machines and Central Impression machines. Unit-type machines feature individual printing stations, allowing for flexibility and modularity; they are ideal for short to medium runs. In contrast, Central Impression machines utilize a single cylindrical impression roller, providing high-quality prints along with improved registration and consistency, making them suitable for longer print runs. Both types cater to diverse printing needs in packaging and labeling applications, driving market growth.</p></p>
