<p><p>The Automotive Active Seatbelt is an advanced safety feature designed to enhance occupant protection by actively adjusting seatbelt tension during critical driving situations, such as sudden braking or collisions. Unlike traditional seatbelts, active seatbelts use sensors and actuators to pre-tension the belt, reducing occupant movement and minimizing injury risks. This technology is increasingly integrated with advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous driving technologies, making it a key component in modern vehicle safety systems.</p><p>The Automotive Active Seatbelt Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising consumer demand for enhanced vehicle safety, stringent government regulations, and advancements in automotive technology.
