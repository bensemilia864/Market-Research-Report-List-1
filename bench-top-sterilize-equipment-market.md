<p><p>The bench-top sterilizer equipment market comprises various types, including steam, plasma, hot air, and others. Steam sterilizers utilize pressurized steam to effectively eliminate pathogens, making them ideal for medical and laboratory applications. Plasma sterilizers employ low-temperature hydrogen peroxide plasma to sterilize sensitive instruments without moisture. Hot air sterilizers use dry heat for sterilization, suitable for heat-resistant items. Other sterilization methods may include chemical or radiation techniques, catering to diverse needs in laboratories, hospitals, and research facilities.</p></p>
