<p><p>The biliary stent systems market comprises various types tailored for specific therapeutic needs. Conventional uncovered stents are designed for temporary bile duct obstruction relief, offering simplicity and ease of placement but lacking long-term patency. Hybrid weaving-type stents combine features of uncovered and covered stents, enhancing durability and reducing the risk of tumor ingrowth while maintaining adequate bile flow. These variations cater to different clinical scenarios, emphasizing the importance of stent design in managing biliary conditions effectively.</p></p>
