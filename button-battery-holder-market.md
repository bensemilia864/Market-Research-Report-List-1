<p><p>The button battery holder market is segmented into three main types: surface mount, through hole, and others. Surface mount holders are designed for mounting directly onto the surface of a circuit board, offering compactness and efficient assembly. Through hole holders feature pins that pass through the board, providing robust mechanical support and stable connections. The "others" category includes specialized variants, such as adjustable or modular holders. Each type serves distinct applications in consumer electronics, medical devices, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
