<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Buzzer Driver Chip market is primarily categorized into two types: electromagnetic and piezoelectric. Electromagnetic buzzers utilize a coil and diaphragm to produce sound through electromagnetic induction, offering a rich tone and a wide frequency range, making them ideal for alarms and notifications. Conversely, piezoelectric buzzers generate sound using piezoelectric materials that vibrate under electrical signals, providing compact, reliable, and energy-efficient solutions suitable for portable devices and applications requiring miniaturization. Each type serves distinct applications based on sound requirements and design constraints.</p></p>
