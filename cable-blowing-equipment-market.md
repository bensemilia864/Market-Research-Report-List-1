<p><ul><li>Hydraulically</li><li>Pneumatically</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cable blowing equipment market consists of three main types: hydraulically, pneumatically, and electrically operated systems. Hydraulically powered equipment uses fluid pressure to push cables into conduits, providing strong force for larger cables. Pneumatically operated equipment relies on compressed air, making it suitable for various environments and easier to maneuver. Electric cable blowing systems use electric motors for precise control and efficiency, often featuring advanced technology for optimal speed and performance. Each type caters to specific installation needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
