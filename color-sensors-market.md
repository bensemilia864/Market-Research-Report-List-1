<p><p>The color sensors market is primarily divided into two types: Monocolor Sensors and RGB Color Sensors. Monocolor Sensors are designed to detect and measure a single color, making them ideal for applications that require specific color identification. In contrast, RGB Color Sensors can detect and analyze the red, green, and blue components of light, allowing for a broader range of color recognition and differentiation. These sensors are used in various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and consumer electronics for quality control and automation.</p></p>
