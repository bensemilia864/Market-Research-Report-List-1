<p><p>The construction insulation materials market includes various types designed to enhance energy efficiency and comfort. Glass wool, made from recycled glass fibers, offers thermal and acoustic insulation. Rockwool, or stone wool, is fire-resistant and sound-absorbent. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is lightweight and moisture-resistant, while Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) provides high compressive strength and moisture resistance. Polyurethane foams offer superior insulation in a lightweight form, and Polyisocyanurate combines thermal performance with fire resistance. Phenolic foams are known for low thermal conductivity. Other materials may include cellulose and aerogels.</p></p>
