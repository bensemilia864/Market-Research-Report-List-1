<p><strong>The Containment Barrier Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Isolator</li><li>Flexible Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The containment barrier isolator market comprises two primary types: rigid isolators and flexible isolators. Rigid isolators are characterized by their sturdy, fixed structures that provide a stable environment for handling hazardous materials while ensuring maximum protection. In contrast, flexible isolators utilize inflatable or adaptable components, allowing for easier access and manipulation of products without compromising safety. Both types are crucial in pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and chemical industries for maintaining containment integrity and preventing contamination during sensitive processes.</p></p>
