<p><p>The Digital Demodulator IC market is categorized into single channel and dual channel types. Single channel digital demodulators are designed to process one signal at a time, making them suitable for applications with minimal complexity and lower data rates. In contrast, dual channel digital demodulators can handle two signals simultaneously, enhancing data processing efficiency and enabling higher throughput. This dual capability makes them ideal for more demanding communication applications, such as telecommunications and broadcasting, where multiple channels are crucial.</p></p>
