<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Communication Controller (EVCC) market comprises two primary types: wired and wireless. Wired communication employs protocols like Power Line Communication (PLC) or Ethernet, offering stable, high-speed connections between electric vehicles and charging stations. Conversely, wireless communication utilizes technologies such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and cellular networks, enabling greater flexibility and mobility for data exchange without physical connections. Both types facilitate vital communication for charging control, grid integration, and vehicle-to-everything (V2X) applications, enhancing overall EV infrastructure efficiency.</p></p>
