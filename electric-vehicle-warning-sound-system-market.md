<p><ul><li>Active Warning Sound System</li><li>Passive Warning Sound System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Warning Sound System market includes **Active Warning Sound Systems** and **Passive Warning Sound Systems**. Active systems generate artificial sounds at low speeds to alert pedestrians, using speakers and software to mimic engine noise. Passive systems rely on natural sounds like tire or wind noise, requiring no additional components. Active systems are more common due to regulatory requirements for pedestrian safety, while passive systems are simpler and cost-effective but less effective in quiet environments. Both aim to enhance safety for silent electric vehicles.</p></p>
