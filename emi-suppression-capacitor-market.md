<p><p>The EMI Suppression Capacitor market is divided into Class X and Class Y capacitors. Class X capacitors are designed for use across power lines, providing a path for high-frequency noise while handling voltage surges. They are typically used in applications like power supplies. Class Y capacitors, on the other hand, are intended for line-to-ground connections, offering protection against electrical shock and ensuring safety in consumer electronics. Both types play critical roles in filtering electromagnetic interference and enhancing device performance.</p></p>
