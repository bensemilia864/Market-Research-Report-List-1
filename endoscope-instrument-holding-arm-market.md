<p><p>The Endoscope Instrument Holding Arm market includes two primary types: Articulated and Rail-mounted. Articulated arms feature multiple joints, offering flexible, multi-directional movement for precise positioning of endoscopes during procedures. They are ideal for dynamic adjustments in complex surgeries. Rail-mounted arms are fixed to a rail system, providing stable, linear movement along a track, suitable for consistent positioning in controlled environments. Both types enhance surgical efficiency, reduce fatigue, and improve precision, catering to diverse medical needs and procedural requirements.</p></p>
