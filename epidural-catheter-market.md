<p><p>The epidural catheter market is segmented into two main types: continuous epidural catheters and single-dose epidural catheters. In contrast, single-dose epidural catheters deliver a one-time injection of medication for immediate pain relief, typically used in shorter procedures. Each type caters to distinct patient needs and procedural requirements within the healthcare setting.</p></p>
