<p><p>The fingerprint reader market can be categorized into USB Type and Integration fingerprint readers. USB Type fingerprint readers are standalone devices that connect to computers or other systems via USB ports, offering easy installation and portability. In contrast, Integration fingerprint readers are embedded into existing devices, such as smartphones, tablets, or access control systems, providing seamless user experiences and enhanced security. Each type serves specific use cases, with USB readers suited for individual use and integrated readers catering to broader applications.</p></p>
