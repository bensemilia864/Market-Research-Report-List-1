<p><p>The flexible shafts market is primarily divided into unidirectional and bidirectional types. Unidirectional flexible shafts transmit torque in a single direction, making them suitable for applications where movement is limited to one way, such as in rotary tools. In contrast, bidirectional flexible shafts allow for torque transmission in both directions, enhancing versatility and efficiency in applications like automotive and machinery where reversing motion is required. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, driving their market demand.</p></p>
