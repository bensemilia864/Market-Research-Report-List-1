<p><p>Fluorocarbon tubing is categorized into several types based on their polymer composition, each with unique properties and applications. PTFE tubing offers excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making it suitable for harsh environments. FEP tubing is transparent and has good flexibility, often used in food and pharmaceutical applications. PFA tubing combines the benefits of PTFE and FEP, providing high purity and excellent flow properties. ETFE tubing is known for its toughness and resistance to radiation, making it ideal for industrial use. Other types may include specialized fluoropolymers tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
