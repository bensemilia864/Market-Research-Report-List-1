<p><p>The half mask respirator market is categorized into three main types: N-series, P-series, and R-series. N-series respirators are not resistant to oil and are effective against non-oil particulates. P-series are oil-proof and suitable for environments with oil-based hazards. R-series respirators offer a limited resistance to oil and are ideal for mixed particulate exposure. Each type caters to specific hazards, influencing their applications across industries like construction, manufacturing, and healthcare, where respiratory protection is essential.</p></p>
