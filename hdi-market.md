<p><p>HDI market types refer to advanced printed circuit board configurations. HDI PCB (1+N+1) features one layer of core material with multiple microvias, while HDI PCB (2+N+2) adds another core layer, enhancing routing capabilities and layer stacking. ELIC (Every Layer Interconnection) allows interconnectivity across all layers, enabling denser designs and improved electrical performance. These technologies are essential for compact, high-performance electronic devices, catering to the growing demand for miniaturization in industries like telecommunications and consumer electronics.</p></p>
