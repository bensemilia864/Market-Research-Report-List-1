<p><strong>The Hemodialysis Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis Powder (fluid)</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemodialysis solutions market comprises two primary types: hemodialysis powder (fluid) and peritoneal dialysate. Hemodialysis powder is a concentrated form that, when mixed with sterile water, creates a dialysis solution used in machines to filter waste from the blood during hemodialysis. In contrast, peritoneal dialysate is a fluid used in peritoneal dialysis, a method where the lining of the abdominal cavity acts as a filter to remove waste and excess fluid. Both solutions are vital for treating chronic kidney disease.</p></p>
