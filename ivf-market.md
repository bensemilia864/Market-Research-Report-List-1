<p><p>The IVF market encompasses various specialized techniques to enhance reproductive outcomes. Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) involves directly injecting a single sperm into an egg, improving fertilization chances. Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER) allows the use of cryopreserved embryos for implantation, providing flexibility in treatment timing. Pre-implantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD) screens embryos for genetic disorders before implantation, ensuring healthier pregnancies. Together, these advancements cater to diverse fertility needs and improve success rates in assisted reproductive technologies.</p></p>
