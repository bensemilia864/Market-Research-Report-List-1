<p><strong>The Laminar Flow Hood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Laminar Flow Hood</li><li>Vertical Laminar Flow Hood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminar flow hood market is categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical laminar flow hoods. Horizontal laminar flow hoods have a fan at the back that blows air across the work surface from the back to the front, providing a sterile environment suitable for tasks requiring protection from contamination. In contrast, vertical laminar flow hoods have a fan at the top, directing airflow downwards towards the work surface, which is ideal for larger equipment and preventing cross-contamination. Both types are essential in laboratories and cleanrooms to ensure high levels of aseptic conditions.</p></p>
