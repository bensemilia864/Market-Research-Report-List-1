<p><p>LIM (Liquid Injection Molding) and LSR (Liquid Silicone Rubber) injection machines operate using three main technologies: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Electric machines offer precision and energy efficiency, ideal for high-quality applications. Pneumatic machines utilize compressed air for actuation, providing flexibility and quick cycle times, though with less control. Hydraulic machines are robust and powerful, suited for high-volume production, handling larger molds easily. Each type caters to specific production needs and materials, influencing the overall market landscape.</p></p>
