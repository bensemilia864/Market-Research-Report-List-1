<p><ul><li>Synchronous Motors</li><li>Induction Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-voltage electric motor market is primarily divided into two types: synchronous motors and induction motors. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed, maintaining synchronization with the supply frequency, making them ideal for applications requiring precise control. Induction motors, on the other hand, rely on electromagnetic induction to generate rotation and are widely used due to their robustness and simplicity. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, HVAC, and transportation, contributing to enhanced efficiency and performance in various applications.</p></p>
