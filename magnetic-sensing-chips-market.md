<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Magnetic Sensing Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance Effect)</li><li>GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance Effect)</li><li>TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance Effect)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic sensing chips market comprises three main types based on their sensing principles. AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance) sensors rely on changes in electrical resistance due to magnetic field orientation, making them suitable for applications like automotive and industrial sensing. GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance) sensors utilize significant resistance changes in multilayer materials in the presence of magnetic fields, widely used in data storage. TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance) employs quantum tunneling effects in magnetic junctions, offering higher sensitivity, ideal for modern memory devices and sensor applications.</p></p>
