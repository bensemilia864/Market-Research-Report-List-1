<p><strong>The Marine Data Buoy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drifting Buoy</li><li>Moored Buoy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine data buoy market is primarily divided into two types: drifting buoys and moored buoys. Drifting buoys are designed to float freely with ocean currents, collecting data on temperature, salinity, and wave patterns while providing valuable insights on oceanic conditions. In contrast, moored buoys are anchored to the sea floor, allowing for continuous monitoring of specific locations, offering detailed data on water quality and meteorological conditions. Both types are essential for marine research, weather forecasting, and climate studies.</p></p>
