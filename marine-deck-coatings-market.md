<p><p>Marine deck coatings are categorized into several types based on their composition and application methods. Water-borne coatings utilize water as the primary solvent, offering low VOC emissions and easier cleanup. Solvent-borne coatings contain organic solvents, providing excellent adhesion and durability but with higher VOC levels. Other market types may include powder coatings and high-solid formulations, which combine performance with reduced environmental impact. Each type caters to specific marine environments and regulatory requirements, ensuring optimal protection and aesthetic appeal for marine surfaces.</p></p>
