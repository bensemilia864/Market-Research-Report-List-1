<p><p>The MEMS mirror chips market is categorized into two primary types: 1-axis scan mirrors and 2-axis scan mirrors. 1-axis scan mirrors are designed to deflect light in a single direction, making them suitable for applications like laser scanning and barcode readers. In contrast, 2-axis scan mirrors can deflect light in two dimensions, enabling more complex scanning tasks such as 3D imaging and projection systems. Both types are integral to advancing technologies in telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics sectors.</p></p>
