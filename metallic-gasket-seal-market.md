<p><p>The metallic gasket and seal market encompasses various types, including spiral wound and metal jacketed gaskets, among others. Spiral wound gaskets consist of alternating layers of metal and filler material, providing excellent sealing performance under high pressure and temperature. Metal jacketed gaskets feature a core of soft material encased in a metallic jacket, enhancing durability and sealing capabilities. Other types may include rigid or soft metallic seals, each designed for specific applications in industrial processes, ensuring effective leakage prevention and reliability.</p></p>
