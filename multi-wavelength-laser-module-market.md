<p><strong>The Multi Wavelength Laser Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode</li><li>Single-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi Wavelength Laser Module market is primarily divided into two types: multimode and single-mode lasers. Multimode lasers support multiple light paths, allowing for higher power output and broader bandwidth. They are often used in applications like data communication and sensing. In contrast, single-mode lasers focus on a single light path, offering higher precision and longer distance transmission, making them ideal for telecommunications and high-frequency applications. Each type addresses specific needs based on range, application, and efficiency.</p></p>
