<p><p>The nerve repair biomaterial market comprises several key types, including nerve conduits, nerve grafts, nerve wraps, and others. Nerve conduits are artificial materials that guide regenerating nerve fibers across gaps. Nerve grafts involve transplanting segments of nerve tissue, often from the patient, to facilitate repair. Nerve wraps are protective materials that encase damaged nerves to promote healing and prevent further injury. Other materials may include bioactive substances or scaffolds, enhancing nerve regeneration through various innovative approaches.</p></p>
