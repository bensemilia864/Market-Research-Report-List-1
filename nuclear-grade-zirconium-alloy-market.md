<p><strong>The Nuclear Grade Zirconium Alloy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sponge</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear grade zirconium alloy market is primarily segmented into two types: sponge and powder. Sponge zirconium is produced through the reduction of zirconium tetrafluoride and is characterized by its high purity, making it suitable for nuclear applications where corrosion resistance and minimal neutron absorption are critical. Powdered zirconium alloy is created by milling sponge zirconium, offering enhanced versatility for various manufacturing processes such as additive manufacturing and sintering. Both forms are essential for producing nuclear reactor components and fuel cladding.</p></p>
