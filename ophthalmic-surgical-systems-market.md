<p><p>The ophthalmic surgical systems market is primarily categorized into two types: laser ophthalmic surgical systems and phacoemulsification ophthalmic surgical systems. Laser ophthalmic surgical systems utilize advanced laser technology for procedures like LASIK and retinal surgery, offering precision and minimal invasiveness. In contrast, phacoemulsification surgical systems are used in cataract surgeries, where ultrasound technology breaks up cataracts, allowing for their removal through a small incision. Both types enhance surgical outcomes and improve patient recovery times in vision correction and restoration procedures.</p></p>
