<p><p>A plate beamsplitter is an optical device used to split a beam of light into two separate paths, typically by reflecting a portion of the light and transmitting the rest. It consists of a thin, flat glass plate coated with a partially reflective material, allowing it to divide light based on wavelength, polarization, or intensity. Plate beamsplitters are widely used in applications such as interferometry, microscopy, laser systems, and telecommunications.</p><p>The Plate Beamsplitter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.1% during the forecast period.
