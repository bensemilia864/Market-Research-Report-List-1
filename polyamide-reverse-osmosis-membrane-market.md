<p><p>A Polyamide Reverse Osmosis (RO) Membrane is a thin-film composite membrane widely used in water purification and desalination processes. It consists of a polyamide layer that acts as a semi-permeable barrier, allowing water molecules to pass while rejecting dissolved salts, contaminants, and impurities. This technology is highly efficient, durable, and cost-effective, making it a preferred choice for industrial, municipal, and residential water treatment applications.</p><p>The Polyamide Reverse Osmosis Membrane Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for clean water, rapid industrialization, and stringent environmental regulations.
