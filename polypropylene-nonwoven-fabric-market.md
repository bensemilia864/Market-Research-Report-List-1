<p><strong>The Polypropylene Nonwoven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbonded Fabric</li><li>Staples Fabric</li><li>Melt Blown Fabric</li><li>Composite Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polypropylene Nonwoven Fabric market includes several types: Spunbonded Fabric, made by extruding molten polymer into continuous filaments, offering strength and durability; Staples Fabric, created by bonding short fibers, providing softness and bulkiness; Melt Blown Fabric, produced by blowing molten polymer into fine fibers, known for filtration and barrier properties; and Composite Fabric, a combination of multiple nonwoven layers, offering enhanced functionality like strength, absorbency, or filtration. Each type caters to diverse applications, from hygiene products to industrial uses.</p></p>
