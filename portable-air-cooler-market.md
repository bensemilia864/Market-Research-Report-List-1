<p><p>A portable air cooler, also known as an evaporative cooler, is a compact, energy-efficient device designed to lower ambient temperatures through the evaporation of water. These coolers work by drawing warm air through water-saturated pads, resulting in cooled and humidified air that is then circulated back into the environment. Their mobility and ease of use make them an attractive alternative to traditional air conditioning systems, especially in regions with low humidity levels.</p><p>The Portable Air Cooler Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period, driven by increasing consumer demand for energy-efficient cooling solutions and rising awareness regarding environmental sustainability.
