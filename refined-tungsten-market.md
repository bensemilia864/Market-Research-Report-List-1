<p><p>The refined tungsten market primarily revolves around three key minerals: wolframite, scheelite, and ferberite. Wolframite, a tungsten-iron-manganese oxide, is valued for its high tungsten content and is commonly used in heavy alloys and industrial applications. Scheelite, a calcium tungstate, is prized for its purity and is widely used in lighting and electronics. Ferberite, an iron-rich tungsten mineral, is essential for high-strength materials and cutting tools. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, driving demand in sectors like aerospace, mining, and manufacturing.</p></p>
