<p><p>The refinish paints market includes solvent-based, water-based, and powder coatings. Solvent-based coatings use organic solvents for durability and smooth finishes, ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, with low VOC emissions, suitable for environments prioritizing sustainability. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and cured under heat, offering excellent durability and resistance, commonly used in metal surfaces. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application requirements in the refinish industry.</p></p>
