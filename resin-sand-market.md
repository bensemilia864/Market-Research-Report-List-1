<p><strong>The Resin Sand Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Coated Sand</li><li>Resin Bonded Sand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin sand market primarily comprises two types: resin-coated sand and resin-bonded sand. Resin-coated sand involves sand particles coated with a thermosetting resin, enhancing strength and stability in casting applications, particularly in metal foundries. Conversely, resin-bonded sand utilizes a resin to bind sand grains without the need for a coating, providing excellent mold precision and durability. Both types cater to industries like automotive and aerospace, where intricate designs and high-quality surface finishes are essential for performance and durability.</p></p>
