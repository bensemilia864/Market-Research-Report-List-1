<p><p>The sandstone market consists of various types, each with distinct characteristics. Quartz sandstone, known for its high durability and resistance to weathering, is primarily used in construction and glass manufacturing. Quartzite, a metamorphic rock formed from quartz sandstone, is valued for its strength and aesthetic appeal, making it ideal for countertops and flooring. Arkose, a feldspar-rich sandstone, is commonly used in concrete and brick production. Each type plays a vital role in diverse industrial applications, driving market demand.</p></p>
