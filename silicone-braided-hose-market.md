<p><p>The Silicone Braided Hose market includes two primary types: Platinum Cured and Peroxide Cured. Platinum Cured hoses are manufactured using a platinum-based catalyst, offering superior purity, high-temperature resistance, and excellent biocompatibility, making them ideal for medical, food, and pharmaceutical applications. Peroxide Cured hoses use organic peroxides for curing, providing robust mechanical properties and cost-effectiveness, suitable for industrial and automotive applications. Both types ensure durability and flexibility, but their curing methods and end-use suitability differentiate their market applications.</p></p>
