<p><p>The SMD thermopile sensor market is segmented into dual channel and single channel sensor modules. Dual channel sensor modules feature two sensing elements, enabling them to measure temperature differences between two surfaces or environments, making them suitable for applications requiring high accuracy or differential measurements. In contrast, single channel sensor modules consist of a single sensing element, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution for general temperature measurement. Both types cater to various industries, including HVAC, medical, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
