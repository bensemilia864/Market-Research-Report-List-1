<p><strong>The Solar Radiation Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicon Cell Type</li><li>Thermopile Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar radiation sensor market comprises two main types: silicon cell type and thermopile type sensors. Silicon cell sensors utilize photovoltaic technology to convert sunlight into electrical energy, offering high sensitivity and quick response times. In contrast, thermopile sensors measure solar radiation by detecting temperature differences caused by absorbed radiation, providing accurate readings over a broad spectrum. Both types serve crucial roles in various applications, including meteorology, renewable energy generation, and environmental monitoring, ensuring effective solar energy management.</p></p>
