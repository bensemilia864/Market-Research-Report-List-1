<p><ul><li>Full Embedding Stimulator</li><li>Semi-embedding Stimulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal cord stimulator market is primarily divided into two types: full embedding stimulators and semi-embedding stimulators. Full embedding stimulators are implanted completely within the body, offering discreetness and reduced risk of migration. In contrast, semi-embedding stimulators have components partially external, allowing easier access for adjustments but presenting a higher visibility. Both types aim to alleviate chronic pain by delivering electrical impulses to the spinal cord, yet differ in their placement, accessibility, and aesthetic appeal for patients.</p></p>
