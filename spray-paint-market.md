<p><strong>The Spray Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray paint market is broadly categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based spray paints use organic solvents as carriers, offering durability, quick drying, and strong adhesion, making them ideal for industrial and outdoor applications. Water-based spray paints use water as a carrier, providing eco-friendliness, low odor, and easier cleanup, suitable for indoor and decorative uses. Both types cater to diverse needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application-specific requirements in construction, automotive, and artistic industries.</p></p>
