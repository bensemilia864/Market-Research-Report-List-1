<p><p>The Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) market includes various types, primarily Styrenic Block Copolymers (TPS or TPE-S) and Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV). TPE-S consists of styrene and elastomer blocks, offering flexibility and durability, ideal for applications like automotive and consumer goods. TPV combines rubber with plastic, creating a material that maintains elasticity while achieving higher heat resistance and chemical stability, making it suitable for demanding applications. Other TPE types encompass various formulations catering to specific industry needs, enhancing versatility across markets.</p></p>
