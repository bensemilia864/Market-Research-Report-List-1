<p><strong>The Thermostatic Control Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermostatic Mixing Valves</li><li>Thermostatic Diverting Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermostatic control valve market comprises two main types: thermostatic mixing valves and thermostatic diverting valves. Thermostatic mixing valves blend hot and cold water to achieve a desired temperature, ensuring safe and consistent water delivery for various applications. In contrast, thermostatic diverting valves direct fluid flow between multiple outlets based on temperature, optimizing system efficiency and performance. Both types are essential in HVAC, plumbing, and industrial settings, enhancing comfort, energy efficiency, and safety in fluid management systems.</p></p>
