<p><strong>The Wafer Handling Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmosheric Transfer Robot</li><li>Vacuum Transfer Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer handling robots market consists of two primary types: Atmospheric Transfer Robots and Vacuum Transfer Robots. Atmospheric Transfer Robots operate in standard atmospheric conditions, relying on mechanical grippers to handle semiconductor wafers, making them suitable for environments with controlled air quality. In contrast, Vacuum Transfer Robots utilize suction mechanisms to grip wafers securely, reducing the risk of contamination or damage. Both types are essential for automating the transfer of wafers within semiconductor fabrication processes, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
